<question collapsible:close title:"What are points? Can I buy permanent plans with them?">
As mentioned above, you earn points by checking in daily. You can also [buy points](https://panel.chmlfrp.net/shop/top-up) at a ratio of 1 RMB : 3000 points.
No, permanent plans cannot be purchased — they can only be redeemed for monthly plans.
</question>

See the [node status page](https://panel.chmlfrp.net/tunnel/status) for details.
</question>
